// PLATBOOK

Platbook — "Shazam for Food"

International travel introduces a massive daily friction: deciphering local, text-only restaurant menus. Platbook is a mobile-first, multi-modal AI application engineered as the "Shazam for food"—the world's first platform allowing users to instantly SEE dishes before ordering. By combining high-speed edge OCR, context-aware localized LLM translation, and real-time visual generation/sourcing pipeline, users can scan any foreign-language menu. Platbook instantly overlays intuitive, high-fidelity images of the dishes, auto-categorizes the plates (e.g., allergens, dietary styles), and appends descriptive culinary context. It bridges the gap between structured AI vision and localized cultural discovery, turning a stressful dining experience into a frictionless, visual delight.
